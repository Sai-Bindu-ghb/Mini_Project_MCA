The exam conducting body, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur has released the information brochure for
JEE Advanced 2025 and Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT) 2025. The JEE Advanced exam will be held on May 18,
2025, with registration starting on April 23 and ending on May 2. Candidates can pay the fee until May 5, and the
admit cards will be available from May 11.

The JEE Advanced exam will be conducted in two sessions: Paper 1 from 9 am to noon, and Paper 2 from 2:30
pm to 5:30 pm. For PwD candidates with at least 40 percent disability, compensatory time will be provided. The
exam is open to candidates born on or after October 1, 2000, with a 5-year relaxation for SC, ST, and PwD
candidates.

Additionally, the AAT 2025 will take place on June 5 for eligible JEE Advanced candidates seeking admission to
the BArch programme at select IITs. Registration for AAT will open on June 2 and close on June 3. Candidates
must carry their JEE Advanced admit card for the AAT exam.
