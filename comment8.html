The passing-out ceremony of the 50th batch of the graduate marine engineers of the Cochin
Shipyard Marine Engineering Training Institute was held on the institute campus on Friday.
Mohandas distributed prizes to the cadets who excelled in various disciplines.

Suresh Parameswara Kurup, managing director of Synergy Oceanic Private Limited, was the
chief guest on the occasion. Representatives of the Cochin Shipyard management, shipping
companies, and faculty members were among those who were present on the occasion, acc-
ording to a release.

Forty marine engineers of the passing-out batch have secured placements from leading ship
-ping companies. The next batch will start on January 1, 2025 with an intake of 40 and 100%
sponsorship from shipping
companies, it said.
